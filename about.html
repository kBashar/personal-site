About

A bit about who I am, what I've worked on, and what I care about.

I'm Khyrul Bashar (Jubayer) — a senior backend engineer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I've spent the last six years building Python-based systems across IoT automation, AI-powered SaaS, and industrial observability — from embedded device backends to LLM-orchestrated pipelines shipping to production in Tokyo.

My arc has gone from systems programming (GSoC at Apache Foundation) through IoT R&D leadership, into the Gen-AI/RAG space. I'm drawn to the intersection of reliable infrastructure and emerging AI capabilities — making sure the "magic" is actually grounded in systems that don't page you at 3 AM.

I care about observability, clean API contracts, and documentation that survives the original author leaving. Open to backend-heavy, SRE, and DevOps roles where those values matter.
